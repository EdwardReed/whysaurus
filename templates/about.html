Mission

Whysaurus seeks to collect, refine and display the world's bext explanations of why.

Team

Whysaurus was conceived and created by Aaron Lifshin and Joshua Frankel.

Joshua Frankel

Creative Director

Joshua is an artist, animator and filmmaker with a brain arranged in a tree-like structure. He grew up in Hell's Kitchen, listening to hip-hop and trying not let anyone take his lunch money. His films have screened in countries like France and have been called "Gorgeous" by someone who writes for The New Yorker. He creates public art with his wife Eve Biddle in New York City and beyond. His website is here.

Aaron Lifshin

CTO

Aaron is a dancing philosopher, programmer and social engineer. Trained in logic and rigor in the elementary grades of the Soviet school system, Aaron immigrated to the US in the late 80's. Since then, he has eschewed dogma and strives to see value in all points of view. Aaron has worked with online technologies in both hands on and management roles since 1999 in Boston and London, UK, and is now settled in San Francisco.
